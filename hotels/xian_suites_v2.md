## Notes

### True 2-BR suites are scarce in central Xi'an

Genuine two-bedroom suites (two separate, lockable bedrooms under one booking) are genuinely rare within the ancient city walls. Most Xi'an hotels at the 4–5★ level offer only single rooms or 1-BR suites; "family rooms" typically put multiple beds in one large room rather than dividing into separate bedrooms. The five options above reflect this market reality:

| Option | True 2-BR? | Within budget? | Walkable to Bell Tower? |
|---|---|---|---|
| HeSu Hidden Art (4★) | Yes (75 m², confirmed) | Yes (~£55–80/night) | 22-min walk / 5-min taxi |
| Hilton Xi'an Presidential (5★) | Yes (248 m², confirmed) | No (est. £200–350/night) | 30-min walk / 5-min taxi |
| Hilton Garden Inn Bell Tower connecting (4★) | Yes (connecting, guaranteed) | Yes (~£80–110/night) | 5-min walk |
| Sofitel Prestige Suite (5★) | No (1-BR + lounge only) | Suite only just within; 2-room combo over limit | 15-min walk |
| Grand Mercure Family Suite (4★) | No (one open room) | Yes (~£60–90/night) | 20-min walk |

### Best fallback if true 2-BR suites sell out

Book **HeSu Hidden Art** first (best confirmed 2-BR option within budget). If unavailable, the **Hilton Garden Inn Bell Tower connecting rooms** is the next best option — guaranteed adjacency via Hilton's confirmed programme, best location, and within budget. The Grand Mercure Family Suite is the budget safety net if privacy between sleeping areas is acceptable.

### Booking.com vs Trip.com note

These dates (Sun 24 – Wed 27 May 2026) fall in late-May domestic tourism high season in China. Trip.com (Ctrip) tends to show lower CNY-denominated rates for Chinese domestic hotels. Cross-check both platforms before booking. Prices above are estimates based on current inventory signals and should be verified at time of booking.

### Connecting rooms at Hilton (option 3)

Hilton's Confirmed Connecting Rooms must be booked via Hilton.com or the Hilton Honors app at least 3 days before check-in. They cannot be booked through Booking.com or other OTAs. After booking, confirm in writing with the hotel that both rooms are assigned as a connecting pair.
