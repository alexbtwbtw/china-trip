# Xi'an — 2-bedroom suites (2 nights, Mon 25 – Wed 27 May 2026)

## Recommendations

### 1. Citadines Central Xi'an — Two-Bedroom Apartment
- **Stars:** 4★ aparthotel (Ascott / Citadines brand)
- **Unit type:** 2-bedroom, 2-bathroom serviced apartment (7 such units in the building; two separate lockable bedrooms + living/dining + kitchenette)
- **Area:** 36 Zhubashi, Beilin District — 0.2 km / ~3 min walk to Bell Tower, ~5 min to Drum Tower, ~7 min to Muslim Quarter, 0.5 km to South Gate of the City Wall. Bang in the middle of the old city.
- **Approx. price/night:** ~£95–120 (CNY 850–1,100) for the 2-BR unit on these dates. Studios list from CNY 420 (~£45); the 2-BR is typically 2–2.5× that, and late-May peak pushes the top of the range. Aiming for the lower end requires booking early on Trip.com.
- **Beds/capacity:** Sleeps 4 (2 separate bedrooms, typically 1 king + 1 twin or 2 queens), 2 bathrooms, fully fitted kitchenette, washer/dryer.
- **Why:** The only genuine "two separate bedrooms under one booking" product that is actually inside the old city and walkable to everything. Serviced-apartment format means real kitchen and laundry — useful on a multi-city China trip. Guests consistently praise the location on Trip.com.

  - Trip.com: https://us.trip.com/hotels/list?city=80&checkIn=2026-05-25&checkOut=2026-05-27&searchKeyword=Citadines%20Central%20Xi%27an

### 2. Eastern House Xi'an (Near Bell & Drum Tower City Wall) — Two-Bedroom Deluxe Suite
- **Stars:** 4★ boutique
- **Unit type:** Two-Bedroom Deluxe Suite, ~100 sqm, with two separate sleeping rooms (typical config: 1 king bed room + 1 room with 2 single/double beds) plus a shared living area
- **Area:** Inside the city walls, ~5 min walk to the City Wall, ~10 min to Drum Tower and Muslim Quarter. Same hotel already on the single-room shortlist in `xian.md`.
- **Approx. price/night:** ~£85–110 (CNY 780–1,000) for the 2-BR suite on these dates — the single-room base starts around £45, and the 2-BR suite runs roughly 2× that. Broadly in budget, with some peak-season upside risk.
- **Beds/capacity:** Sleeps 4 adults comfortably; good for a family or two couples
- **Why:** 9.4/10 on Trip.com across 6,370+ reviews — the best-reviewed option of the three. Rich breakfast buffet, free afternoon tea, boutique feel. Most atmospheric of the options and the closest to the Muslim Quarter food scene.

### 3. Novotel Xi'an The Bell Tower — Two Interconnecting Rooms

- **Unit type:** Two interconnecting/adjoining rooms booked together (sold as a package by the hotel for families). Each is a full separate guestroom with its own bathroom, linked by an internal door. Meets the "connecting rooms sold as a package" brief.
- **Area:** Directly adjacent to the Bell Tower, Muslim Quarter, and City Wall — the most central address on this list.

Request the interconnecting pair explicitly at booking (note: "connecting rooms on request, not guaranteed until confirmed by the hotel").

## Notes
- **Peak season caveat:** Late May is warm, dry, domestic-tourism high season in Xi'an — expect 15–30% uplift vs. shoulder rates, and another bump if the booking falls near a Chinese public holiday. The £90/night target is tight for a true 2-BR product; Eastern House is the most likely to come in at budget, Citadines slightly above, Novotel above. Book early.
- **Connecting-room rule:** At Novotel (and any hotel sold as "interconnecting"), the connecting door is subject to availability and only *guaranteed* once the hotel emails confirmation. Always follow up by email within 48 hours of booking, referencing both reservation numbers, asking to be blocked as connecting.
- **Candidates ruled out:**
  - *Holiday Inn Express Bell Tower* — their "family room" is a single room with 2 queen beds, not 2 separate bedrooms; does not meet the brief.
  - *Grand Mercure on Renmin Square* — the only 2-bedroom product is the Presidential Suite (well over £200/night).
  - *Fraser Suites Xi'an* — no such property exists in Xi'an (Frasers operates in Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Beijing, etc., not Xi'an).
  - *Somerset Grand Central Xi'an* — no such property; the closest Somerset is *Somerset Xindicheng Xi'an*, which does have 2-BR apartments but is ~16 min drive (south of the city, off South 2nd Ring Rd) from Bell Tower — fails the "inside the walls" requirement.
- **Platform tip:** Trip.com (Ctrip) consistently beats Booking.com on Chinese domestic properties and often shows lower-rate 2-BR inventory. Cross-check both before locking in.
