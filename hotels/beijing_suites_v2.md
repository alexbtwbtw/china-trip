**The £90/night target is not achievable for a true 2-BR unit in Wangfujing/Dongcheng in May 2026.** The hard limit of £130/night is borderline — it may only be reached by the Park Plaza Two-Bedroom Family Room (Pick 5) with an early advance non-refundable rate, and even then it is not guaranteed. The reality for the Beijing 4–5★ 2-BR market:

| Option | Approx. nightly rate (whole unit) | vs. Budget |
|---|---|---|
| Park Plaza 2-BR Family Room (60 m²) | ~£115–£160 | Borderline / over |
| Park Plaza 2-BR Family Suite (90 m²) | ~£150–£230 | Over hard limit |
| Imperial Mansion Marriott 2-BR Suite | ~£160–£210 | Over hard limit |
| Ascott Raffles City 2-BR Executive | ~£155–£185 | Over hard limit |

For comparison, the previous research found that **base-Beijing Wangfujing** (now excluded) is the only Dongcheng 2-BR option regularly seen near £90–£115 — precisely because it is an independent hutong-style property rather than a branded hotel.

**If the budget is strict at £130**, the realistic options are:
1. Book two separate standard rooms at a Wangfujing 4★ (see `beijing_bedrooms.md` — Guo Ji Yi Yuan/Park Plaza standard rooms, or Sunworld Hotel Wangfujing) and request adjacent/connecting allocation.
2. Accept the Park Plaza Two-Bedroom Family Room (Pick 5) if the live rate on Booking.com falls under £130 on your booking date — check early, as May is relatively low season.
3. Extend the search to Chaoyang serviced apartments further from Wangfujing (Yonganli/Jianguomen area, 2–3 stops on Line 1) where 2-BR serviced apartments occasionally list in the £100–£130 range.

### Metro note: "≤2 stops from Wangfujing"

- **Picks 1, 2, 3, 5** (Park Plaza, Imperial Mansion, Lee Garden): All within Wangfujing/Dongcheng, walking distance from Dengshikou (Line 5) or Wangfujing (Line 1/8) stations. Zero metro required for Wangfujing Street access.
- **Pick 4** (Ascott Raffles City): Dongzhimen — 4 station stops and one line-change to Wangfujing (~13 min total by metro). Technically exceeds the ≤2-stop criterion but is fast and the Airport Express connection at Dongzhimen is a significant practical advantage.
- **Excluded — Ascott Beijing Jianguo Road**: Yonganli metro (Line 1), 3 stops from Wangfujing — exceeds the criterion.
- **Excluded — Jingguang / Hujialou area**: 12+ stops from Wangfujing — far too remote.
- **Excluded — Somerset Grand Fortune Garden / Fraser Suites CBD**: Both in Liangmaqiao/CBD, 5+ km from Wangfujing, and both confirmed over £130 for 2-BR units.
- **Excluded — Grand Skylight CIMC**: The branded property matching this name is in Yizhuang (Daxing District) or the Olympic area — not in Wangfujing/Dongcheng; not suitable.

### Foreign-passport check-in

All five properties routinely accept foreign guests. Chinese regulations require hotels to register foreign passport details with local police (public security bureau) at check-in — all the branded properties (Marriott, Ascott, Park Plaza) handle this automatically as a matter of course. Send the property an email/WeChat confirmation 1–2 weeks before arrival with your passport numbers and nationality to pre-register and speed up check-in. For Lee Garden, confirm via email that the property's short-stay licence for foreign-passport guests is current (this is rarely an issue at a 5★ Wangfujing property but worth confirming).
