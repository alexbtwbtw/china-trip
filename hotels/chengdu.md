# Chengdu — 2 nights (Fri 22 – Sun 24 May 2026)

## Recommendations

### 1. Mercure Chengdu Chunxi (Taikoo Li)
- **Stars:** 4★
- **Area:** Jinjiang district, 3–5 min walk to Chunxi Road Pedestrian Street and Taikoo Li
- **Approx. price/night:** £45 (~$57 / 410 RMB)
- **Why:** Trip.com-ranked #1 4-star select hotel in Chengdu with 9.6/10 from 22,000+ reviews. Accor-brand reliability, sits right between Chunxi Road and Taikoo Li — hard to beat on location.
- **Book:** https://www.trip.com/hotels/chengdu-hotel-detail-21132716/mercure-chengdu-chunxi/?checkIn=2026-05-22&checkOut=2026-05-24

### 2. Lyhn International Hotel Chengdu Taikoo Li
- **Stars:** 4★
- **Area:** No. 1 Tidu Street, Qingyang side of Taikoo Li — ~600 m to Chunxi Road, ~900 m to Taikoo Li (7 min walk)
- **Approx. price/night:** £38 (~$48 / 345 RMB)
- **Why:** Cheapest of the three and rated #2 4-star in Chengdu on Trip.com (9.6/10 from 11,000+ reviews). Floor-to-ceiling city views, strong breakfast — excellent value slightly under budget.
- **Book:** https://us.trip.com/hotels/chengdu-hotel-detail-690730/lyhn-international-hotel-chengdu-taikoo-li/?checkIn=2026-05-22&checkOut=2026-05-24

### 3. Atour Hotel Chengdu Taikoo Li Chunxi Road Pedestrian
- **Stars:** 4★
- **Area:** Jinjiang district, 5 min walk to Chunxi Road, 400 m to Chunxi Road metro (Line 2/3)
- **Approx. price/night:** £48 (~$61 / 440 RMB)
- **Why:** Popular Chinese design-forward chain; 89 modern, soundproofed rooms with the Atour signature lobby library. Walk-out-the-door access to the pedestrian street itself.
- **Book:** https://www.booking.com/hotel/cn/atour-chengdu-taikoo-li-chunxi-road-pedestrian.html?checkin=2026-05-22&checkout=2026-05-24&group_adults=1&no_rooms=1

## Notes
- Prices are indicative "from" rates shown on Trip.com / Booking.com in April 2026 for one guest; actual May 22–24 rates often run 10–20% higher on a Friday/Saturday stay, so budget a small buffer. All three still track within £35–55/night in practice.
- Chinese domestic booking platforms (Trip.com, Ctrip, Fliggy) frequently undercut Booking.com by 10–15% for mainland hotels — worth a price check before confirming.
- Taikoo Li / Chunxi Road is the single best base in Chengdu: metro Line 2/3 interchange, walkable to Daci Temple and People's Park, and 25 min by metro to Chengdu East Railway Station for Leshan/panda bases day trips.
- If Mercure sells out, Hilton Garden Inn Chengdu Chunxi Road Center (9.6/10, ~£50/night, 1.1 km to Taikoo Li) is the closest equivalent backup.
