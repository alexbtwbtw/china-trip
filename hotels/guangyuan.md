# Guangyuan — 1 night (Sun 24 – Mon 25 May 2026)

## Recommendations

### 1. Hampton by Hilton Guangyuan Lizhou Square
- **Stars:** 4★
- **Area:** Lizhou District, No. 1072 Lizhou East Road — on Lizhou Square, central business/government district
- **Approx. price/night:** £33 (~$42 / 300 RMB)
- **Why:** Highest-rated hotel in Guangyuan (9.4/10 "Outstanding" across 3,200+ reviews) and ranked #1 among 4-star selects in the city. Reliable international brand with free hot breakfast, ~5 km to Guangyuan Railway Station — an easy 10-minute taxi for the onward Xi'an leg.
- **Book:** https://us.trip.com/hotels/guangyuan-hotel-detail-25395219/hampton-by-hilton-guangyuan-lizhou-square/?checkIn=2026-05-24&checkOut=2026-05-25

### 2. Wanda Realm Guangyuan
- **Stars:** 5★ (strictly above spec, but included because it comes in on budget)
- **Area:** Wanyuan New Area, Lizhou District — attached to Guangyuan Wanda Plaza (shopping/dining)
- **Approx. price/night:** £50 (~$63 / 450 RMB)
- **Why:** Actual 5-star property (pool, spa, river views, Harman Kardon speakers in rooms) at a small-city price. 9.2/10 review score over 3,700+ reviews. 7–8 km from the railway station; ideal if you want to treat the stopover as a proper reset between Chengdu and Xi'an.
- **Book:** https://us.trip.com/hotels/guangyuan-hotel-detail-1879371/wanda-realm-guangyuan/?checkIn=2026-05-24&checkOut=2026-05-25

### 3. Tiancheng Grand Hotel (广元天成大酒店)
- **Stars:** 4★ (local 5★ classification on some Chinese sites)
- **Area:** No. 60 Caishen Street, Lizhou West Road — riverside old-town side of Lizhou District
- **Approx. price/night:** £42 (~$53 / 380 RMB)
- **Why:** 9.3/10 guest score with a standout "almost 270-degree river view" from deluxe rooms; walking distance to Jialing River promenade restaurants. Swimming pool, gym, sauna. ~20-minute drive to Guangyuan Railway Station (slightly further than the Hampton, but compensates with setting).
- **Book:** https://us.trip.com/hotels/guangyuan-hotel-detail-486693/tiancheng-grand-hotel/?checkIn=2026-05-24&checkOut=2026-05-25

## Notes
- Guangyuan is a small prefecture-level city in northern Sichuan; Booking.com coverage is thin, so Trip.com (Ctrip's international front-end) has the fullest inventory and English reviews. Agoda also lists all three.
- For the onward leg to Xi'an: trains depart from **Guangyuan Railway Station** (广元站) in the south of Lizhou District. The Hampton is the closest of the three (~5 km). Journey time to Xi'an North on G-series high-speed trains is about 2h 40m.
- Guangyuan Fengtai International Hotel exists and sits ~4 km from the station, but rates and reviews are inconsistent across platforms; the three above are safer picks.
- Lizhou Grand Hotel (#6 of 40 on Tripadvisor) is a serviceable older 4★ near Huangze Temple but is being outclassed on price/quality by the Hampton — skipped.
- All prices are indicative from Trip.com listings pulled in April 2026; verify at booking time as small-city hotels swing with local events.
