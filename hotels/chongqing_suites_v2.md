## Notes

**Strongest picks (in order):**
1. **Marriott Executive Apartments Chongqing** — the single best overall: international brand, 2-min walk to Jiefangbei, full-kitchen 2-BR apartment, mid-budget. Replaces the Voyul Guyu Li role as "genuine central 2-BR apartment" but with Marriott standards.
2. **Somerset Jiefangbei Chongqing** — same Ascott operational quality as the already-shortlisted Somerset Yangtze River but 2 minutes from Jiefangbei (no vertical climb). Book this if the Yangtze River property is sold out or if the plateau-level location is preferred.
3. **Roosevelt Service Apartment** — the best-value branded pick, best if hitting the low end of the £70–£180 band matters. Less polished than Marriott/Somerset but genuine 2-BR/2-bath and right in the Jiefangbei CBD.

**Budget-stretch picks:**
- **InterContinental Raffles City (Embassy Suite)** and **INDITION Chongqing (Crystal Pool Duplex)** both break the £180/night cap — included per the brief's explicit candidate list and because they represent the peninsula's two most distinctive 2-BR experiences (IC = brand prestige + confluence views; INDITION = private in-suite pool). Use only if upgrading.

**Location spectrum:**
- Plateau / Liberation Monument side: Marriott Executive Apartments, Somerset Jiefangbei, Roosevelt (all 2-min to 15-min walk, no river-level climb).
- Chaotianmen tip / Raffles City: InterContinental Raffles City, INDITION (both on the Yangtze-Jialing confluence, 2 metro stops to Jiefangbei on Line 1).

**Pricing caveat:** 2-BR inventory on Booking.com is often hidden unless you search with **4 adults, 1 room** — always use `group_adults=4&no_rooms=1` parameters. Trip.com / Agoda / Ctrip consistently show lower RMB-native rates than Booking.com for the Marriott Executive Apartments, Somerset Jiefangbei, and Roosevelt — compare before booking. The IC Embassy Suite and INDITION Crystal Pool Duplex prices above are estimates based on their rarity (12 Crystal Pool units; Embassy Suite typically 1 on floor); actual rates for these dates may flex ±20%.
