## Notes

**Strongest picks:**

1. **Mercure Xi'an The Bell Tower (Hotel 3)** is the top value pick — 50 m from Bell Tower, 4★ Accor, Trip.com 9.6/10, and the lowest May prices on the list (~£40–52). The only material drawback is no Booking.com listing; book via Trip.com or Accor ALL.

2. **Novotel Xi'an The Bell Tower (Hotel 1)** is the safest all-round pick — 9,815 Trip.com reviews at 9.6/10 is the most statistically reliable quality signal available, Metro Line 2 at the front door, and Booking.com 8.8 across a well-established review base. Slightly pricier than the Mercure but well within budget.

3. **Atour X Hotel (Hotel 4)** is the best value when budget is the primary constraint — under £50/night, Booking.com 9.3, 4★, and the Atour X brand reliably delivers above-average rooms for the price. 6-min walk to Bell Tower is the only location trade-off.

**May is peak season:** Post-May Day Golden Week (1–5 May) the Xi'an tourist season runs hot through June. Prices quoted above reflect realistic midweek (Sun–Wed) May rates; weekend rates or last-minute bookings will be higher. Book at least 6–8 weeks in advance.

**Booking two separate rooms:** Hotels 1, 2, and 5 all have 198–214 rooms — two rooms under different guest names is easy. Hotel 3 (Mercure, 113 rooms) and Hotel 4 (Atour X) are smaller; request same floor / adjacent in the booking notes or follow up by email with both reservation numbers. None of these hotels officially advertise interconnecting room availability — treat "adjacent" as achievable, "interconnecting" as unconfirmed.

**Booking.com vs Trip.com note:** For Hotels 1, 2, 4 and 5 the Booking.com link above is the primary booking path. For Hotel 3 (Mercure Bell Tower), Trip.com (hotel ID 55141379) or Accor ALL is the correct channel — the property does not appear to have a Booking.com listing as of April 2026.

Moxy Xi'an Downtown (Booking.com 8.4 — just below 8.5 threshold). JI Hotel Xi'an Bell Drum Towers (3★). Courtyard by Marriott Xi'an North (not in city centre).
