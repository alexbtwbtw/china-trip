# Beijing — 4 nights (Wed 27 – Sun 31 May 2026)

## Recommendations

### 1. Guo Ji Yi Yuan Hotel
- **Stars:** 4★
- **Area:** Wangfujing Street, Dongcheng District (48 Wangfujing Avenue)
- **Approx. price/night:** £46 (from CNY 426 / ~$60)
- **Why:** Directly on the Wangfujing pedestrian shopping street, 5-min walk to Jinyu Hutong metro (Line 8) and roughly 15-min walk to the Forbidden City. The only candidate that comfortably sits on budget while still 4-star. Review scores 8.1 (Booking) / 8.7 (Trip.com) across ~1,300+ reviews; decor is dated but location is unbeatable for this price.
- **Book:** https://www.booking.com/hotel/cn/crowne-plaza-beijing.html?checkin=2026-05-27&checkout=2026-05-31&group_adults=1&no_rooms=1

### 2. Inner Mongolia Grand Hotel Wangfujing
- **Stars:** 4★
- **Area:** Dongcheng — 2 Chongwenmen Nei St, 800 m from Wangfujing, 200 m to Dongdan metro (Lines 1 & 5)
- **Approx. price/night:** £60 (from ~$77-83)
- **Why:** Highest-rated of the shortlist at 9.0/10 (2,395 reviews) / 8.9 on Kayak. Short walk to Wangfujing and one metro stop from Tiananmen/Forbidden City. Runs ~£15 over budget but buys a big jump in quality (4 restaurants, Mongolian-themed lobby, well-kept rooms).
- **Book:** https://www.booking.com/hotel/cn/inner-mongolia-grand-wangfujing.html?checkin=2026-05-27&checkout=2026-05-31&group_adults=1&no_rooms=1

### 3. Novotel Beijing Peace
- **Stars:** 4★
- **Area:** 3 Jinyu Hutong, Dongcheng — inside the Wangfujing block, 320 m to Jinyu Hutong metro (Line 8)
- **Approx. price/night:** £66 (from ~$84)
- **Why:** Reliable Accor-brand 4★ with a consistent 8.7/10 from 1,756 reviews. Walking distance to Wangfujing Pedestrian Street and ~20-min walk to the Forbidden City. Over budget, but the easiest "no-surprises" choice for a first-time Beijing traveler.
- **Book:** https://www.booking.com/hotel/cn/novotelpeace.html?checkin=2026-05-27&checkout=2026-05-31&group_adults=1&no_rooms=1

## Notes
- Only Guo Ji Yi Yuan lands on the £45 target; true 4★ properties inside the Wangfujing loop generally start at £55-65/night. If budget is firm, drop to Guo Ji Yi Yuan or accept that Happy Dragon Alley (which was on the shortlist) is a 2★ hostel/guesthouse and does not meet the 4★ requirement.
- Check-out 31 May is a Sunday, not Saturday as in the heading above — the stay is still 4 nights (Wed-Sun).
- Book via Trip.com or Ctrip if paying with a Chinese/Alipay method — often 10-15% cheaper than Booking.com for mainland hotels. Otherwise Booking.com links above are fine.
- All three are within 2 metro stops of Tiananmen East (Line 1), which is the entry point for the Forbidden City — plan to arrive before 09:00 to avoid queues.
- Confirm the hotel accepts foreign passports at check-in; some budget/mid-tier Beijing hotels still refuse non-Chinese guests — the three above all do accept foreigners, but reconfirm when booking.
