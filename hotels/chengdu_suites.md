# Chengdu — 2-bedroom suites (2 nights, Fri 22 – Sun 24 May 2026)

Budget target: ~£90/night (~820 RMB / ~$114) for the whole 2-BR unit. Honest caveat up front — a genuine two-bedroom serviced apartment in central Chengdu from a reputable operator typically runs **£110–£180/night** in May, not £90. The options below are ranked by best value + closest-to-budget + reviews + location. All three are serviced apartments (best fit for "two separate bedrooms in one booking") rather than connecting hotel rooms, which are rarely sold as a single bookable package in mainland China.

## Notes
- **Budget reality check:** A real 2-BR apartment from an international operator in central Chengdu is almost always above £90/night. Somerset Riverview is the only one of the three that can realistically land close to budget (sometimes right at it on weekday/early-bird rates). If £90 is a hard ceiling, the traveller will need to either (a) drop to a Chinese-brand 3★ apartment like Summer Island Taikoo Li (9.6/10 on Trip.com, but only 3★ so fails the 4★ requirement), or (b) accept a king-room-with-sofa-bed in a 4★ hotel (not a true second bedroom).
- **Connecting rooms in China:** Mainland Chinese 4–5★ hotels (Niccolo, Temple House, Shangri-La) do offer connecting rooms, but they're almost never sold as a bundled package on Booking.com / Trip.com — you book two separate rooms and phone/email the hotel to request the connecting pair, with no guarantee. For a firm booking of two separate bedrooms, a serviced apartment is the reliable route.
- **Booking platform tip:** Trip.com (Ctrip) usually has the best inventory and price for mainland Chinese properties — worth cross-checking against Booking.com. Agoda is also strong for APAC.
- **Verify at booking:** Prices above are estimates drawn from 2025 data and brand-average rates. Confirm the exact May 22–24 2026 rate on each OTA before booking; rates can shift ±20% based on demand and any current Chengdu events.
- **Chengdu May weather:** Mild and often rainy — Somerset's indoor pool and Ascott's in-mall location are genuine wet-weather perks.
