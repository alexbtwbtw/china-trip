# Chongqing — 3 nights (Tue 19 – Fri 22 May 2026)

## Recommendations

### 1. Walling Hotel (Jiefangbei Hongyadong)
- **Stars:** 4★
- **Area:** Yuzhong — Jialingjiang Binjianglu, walking distance to Hongyadong and Jiefangbei Pedestrian Street
- **Approx. price/night:** £41 (~$52 / ~375 RMB)
- **Why:** Consistently strong 9.6/10 reviews on Trip.com, 8.4+ on Booking.com; 15th-floor rooftop with panoramic city views and genuinely central for both Hongyadong and Liberation Monument.
- **Book:** https://www.booking.com/hotel/cn/walling-hongyadong-jiefangbei-chongqing.html?checkin=2026-05-19&checkout=2026-05-22&group_adults=1&no_rooms=1

### 2. Yuexuan Hotel Chongqing Jiefangbei Hongyadong Branch
- **Stars:** 4★
- **Area:** Yuzhong — 350 m to Xiaoshizi metro, ~13 min walk to Liberation Monument / Jiefangbei
- **Approx. price/night:** £48 (~$60 / ~435 RMB)
- **Why:** 8.9/10 guest score, guests repeatedly praise the clean rooms and river/city views; right on the Hongyadong side so you can walk to the light-up at night.
- **Book:** https://us.trip.com/hotels/chongqing-hotel-detail-127961745/yuexuan-hotel/?checkIn=2026-05-19&checkOut=2026-05-22

### 3. Qianhe Rishan River View Hotel
- **Stars:** 4★
- **Area:** Yuzhong — Xiaoshizi metro 100 m, same building cluster as Chongqing Eye observation deck
- **Approx. price/night:** £38 (~$48 / ~345 RMB)
- **Why:** Excellent 9.5/10 rating across 4,000+ reviews, floor-to-ceiling windows with river/cyberpunk skyline views, and the cheapest of the three while still firmly 4★ and a short walk to Jiefangbei.
- **Book:** https://www.trip.com/hotels/chongqing-hotel-detail-44187808/qianhe-rishan-hotel/?checkIn=2026-05-19&checkOut=2026-05-22

## Notes
- Prices are "from" rates pulled from Trip.com / Booking.com in April 2026 for a standard double; confirm by entering 19–22 May 2026 at checkout — mid-May is shoulder season in Chongqing so rates should hold close to these.
- Chongqing star ratings are self-declared; the three above all read as solid 4★ on international sites (scores 8.9–9.6) rather than true luxury 4★. If you want a more international-brand feel, budget closer to £90–£120/night (Seya Panoramic, ISEYA Flagship).
- All three are in Yuzhong peninsula within ~15 min walk of both Jiefangbei Pedestrian Street and Hongyadong. Walling is closest to Hongyadong; Qianhe Rishan is closest to the metro. Chongqing is extremely hilly — check whether "walking distance" involves stairs/escalators up from the riverfront before committing.
- Booking via Trip.com usually shows lower RMB-native rates than Booking.com for domestic Chinese properties; worth comparing both for each hotel before booking.
