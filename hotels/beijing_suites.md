## Notes

- **Budget reality check.** True 2-bedroom suites in Beijing's 4–5★ inventory mostly sit £180–£350/night — the £90/night target is unrealistic for a single-unit 2-BR suite at Wangfujing-proper branded hotels (Park Plaza, Hilton, Peninsula, Waldorf all far above). Candidates that *do* hit ~£90–£130 are either (a) independent/serviced-apartment brands in Dongcheng hutongs (base-Wangfujing), (b) Chaoyang-side aparthotels one metro stop away (Citadines Ritan, Fraser Suites CBD, Ascott Raffles City — the latter two were all £140+/night for a 2-BR), or (c) connecting-room family packages (Novotel Peace).
- **Properties I considered and dropped:**
  - **Ascott Beijing / Ascott Raffles City Beijing (Dongzhimen):** 2-BR Executive ~€185 (~£160)/night — well over budget.
  - **Somerset Grand Fortune Garden (Liangmaqiao):** Property is largely long-lease (~¥32k/month for 2-BR = ~£35/night if monthly, but nightly short-stay rates quoted ~$200+/night on third-party sites); also Liangmaqiao is ~7 km from Wangfujing and less central than the three picks above.
  - **Fraser Suites CBD:** 2-BR 105 sqm apartments run £150–£200/night — above budget.
  - **Park Plaza Beijing Wangfujing:** Has a "family room" but it is one room, not two separate bedrooms — does not meet the brief.
  - **Inner Mongolia Grand Hotel:** Base rates from ~$77/night are attractive but the "family/suite" types are single-room layouts with extra beds, not true 2-BR — does not meet the brief.
- **Checkout is Sunday 31 May, not Saturday.** Double-check that the booking engine shows 4 nights (27, 28, 29, 30 May) before confirming.
- **Foreign-passport check-in.** All three picks routinely accept foreign guests, but Chinese hotels are required by law to register foreign passports with the local police. Arrive with passport + printed visa/entry stamp; some smaller aparthotels (base-Wangfujing is the borderline case here) occasionally turn away foreigners if their police registration licence lapses — worth sending the property a WeChat/email 1–2 weeks out confirming they accept foreign passports for these dates.
- **4-night discounts.** All three Ascott-family brands (Ascott, Citadines, Somerset) price better when booked direct on discoverasr.com for stays of 4+ nights — always compare direct vs. Booking/Trip.com.
- **Prices above are estimates** based on current OTA ranges for comparable mid-week spring dates; confirm live on Booking.com/Trip.com/Agoda with the 2026-05-26 → 2026-05-31 dates before committing.
