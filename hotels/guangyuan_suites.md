## Notes

**True 2-bedroom suites effectively do not exist in Guangyuan.** Checked all four candidates:

- **Hampton by Hilton Guangyuan Lizhou Square:** Standard-only inventory, no suite SKU at all.
- **Wanda Realm Guangyuan:** Brand standard includes a Family Suite category, but the Guangyuan property's Trip.com/Ctrip inventory is typically limited to Deluxe King, Deluxe Twin, Executive, and a 1-BR Presidential-style suite — no consistent 2-BR SKU. This is the only property where a Family Suite might appear; worth checking at booking time.
- **Tiancheng Grand Hotel:** "Suites" are 1-BR + sitting-room combos; no 2-BR layout.
- **Guangyuan Fengtai International (广元凤台国际大酒店):** Older 4★ near the station, ~4 km away. Room-type data is thin and inconsistent across platforms; reviews are mediocre (sub-8/10). No evidence of a 2-BR suite SKU. Not recommended over the three above.

**Fallback strategy (the realistic answer):** Book **two separate standard rooms at the same hotel**, ideally adjacent/connecting. Request "连通房" (connecting rooms) in the booking notes. Ranked order for this fallback:

1. **Hampton by Hilton Lizhou Square** — most central, highest reviews, cheapest (~£66 for 2 rooms), breakfast included for 4 people.
2. **Wanda Realm Guangyuan** — if the traveller wants the 5★ experience and occasional Family Suite inventory; ~£90 total for 2 rooms stays on budget.
3. **Tiancheng Grand Hotel** — pick if river views matter more than proximity to the station.

All three fallbacks land at or under the £90 budget for two rooms, confirming the "well under budget in a small city" expectation. Trip.com/Ctrip carries the fullest inventory for Guangyuan; Booking.com coverage is thin. For the onward Xi'an leg, Guangyuan Railway Station (广元站) is 5–7 km from all three — Hampton is closest.
